✦ our story ✦

A trip that changed everything

Last year, my family visited Sri Lanka — and I came home a different person. Seeing the children there lit something up in me. I'd been crocheting and knitting for years, and suddenly I knew exactly what all those loops were for: spreading love to kids who needed to feel it most.

I'm so excited to start right there in Sri Lanka, and to do it alongside my cousins Aura and Taytay, who feel the same way I do about putting smiles on children's faces. Together we've been connecting with hospitals and orphanages across Sri Lanka, delivering crocheted and knitted items made by friends, family, and volunteers from around the world.

Every single item is handmade with care, tagged with love, and placed in the hands of a child who deserves to feel seen and cherished. That's what Love and Loops is — one loop at a time.
