🌍 Starting in Sri Lanka

Every child deserves to feel loved

Love and Loops was founded by Alaya at just 16 years old, driven by a simple but powerful belief: that a handmade blanket or toy carries something no factory can replicate — the warmth of human hands and the intention behind every loop.

We began by connecting with hospitals and orphanages in villages across Sri Lanka, bringing crocheted and knitted items made by friends, family, and volunteers from around the world.

Each item is handmade with care, tagged with love, and delivered to children who deserve to feel seen and cherished.
